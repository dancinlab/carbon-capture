# 🜂 H_A002 — Night-frost collector ("밤서리 수집기")

## Concept

Sky-facing radiative-cooling panels dump heat through the atmospheric window to deep space (≈3 K)
to reach CO₂'s frost point and snow CO₂ out of the air — **zero electricity, the heat sink is the
universe**.

## Falsifiable prediction (kernel — not run)

CO₂ frost point at its 400-ppm partial pressure (≈0.42 mbar) is ≈ **−140 °C** (Clausius-Clapeyron
from the 1-atm sublimation point −78.5 °C). Passive radiative cooling reaches at best ≈ **−50 to
−60 °C** (ambient minus ~50 K). **Predict FALSIFIED-in-principle: the ~80 K gap cannot be closed
passively** — radiative cooling alone never reaches the 400-ppm CO₂ frost point. Vindicated only at
already-cryogenic ambient (polar night / high altitude, cf. H_A "ALTITUDE-STILL") or with active
pre-cooling (then it is no longer passive/free).

orthogonal to everything verified.
