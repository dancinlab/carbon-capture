# 🜂 H_A008 — Solar blink ("햇빛 깜빡이 흡착제")

## Concept

A photoswitchable sorbent (azobenzene / spiropyran grafted into the pore) **binds CO₂ in one
photo-isomer and releases it in the other**. Regeneration is a *flash of light*, not a temperature
swing — directly deleting the dominant addressable energy term (H_010 sensible heat) that H_019
identified as 100% of the frontier→floor gap.

## Falsifiable prediction (kernel — not run)

A visible photon is ≈ 2–3 eV ≈ **200–290 kJ/mol**. If isomerization needs **1 photon per CO₂ released**,
the optical cost is WORSE than thermal regeneration (H_010 ~100 kJ/mol). **Predict: viable ONLY if (a)
quantum yield ≫ 1 effective CO₂ per absorbed photon (cooperative/domino switching) OR (b) sunlight is
free and the metric is land/$ not energy.** Kill criterion: photons-per-release × ℏω vs the H_010
sensible heat it replaces. Vindicated if cooperative switching releases many CO₂ per photon.

## Why novel (vs the verified H_0xx chain)

heat — an optical-regeneration axis. Couples naturally to solar (the energy is already photons).
