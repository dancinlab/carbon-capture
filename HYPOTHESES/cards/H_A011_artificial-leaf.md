# 🜂 H_A011 — Artificial leaf ("인공 잎")

## Concept

A photo-electrochemical cell reduces CO₂ (from ambient air or a capture stage) to CO / formate /
methanol using **only sunlight and water** — capture, energy, and conversion (H_008) fused into one
sunlight-driven step. The leaf does what a plant does, faster and to a chosen fuel.

## Falsifiable prediction (kernel — not run)

The reduction floor (H_008) is ~32.8 GJ/ton-C; an artificial leaf supplies it from photons. At ~10%
solar-to-fuel efficiency and ~200 W/m² mean insolation, the area to fix 1 Mt-C/yr is **large but
finite** (~10³–10⁴ km²). **Predict: energetically "free" (sunlight), so the binding constraint is
AREA and catalyst durability, not energy** — the opposite trade from electric DAC. Kill criterion:
solar-to-fuel efficiency × durable catalyst lifetime. Vindicated if efficiency × area beats land-use
alternatives.

## Why novel (vs the verified H_0xx chain)

H_008 showed conversion is energy-DOMINANT on grid power; the artificial leaf pays that energy in
free photons instead — flipping conversion from a cost into a sunlight-harvest. Capture + reduction in
one device, no separate compression (H_011) or thermal regeneration (H_010).
