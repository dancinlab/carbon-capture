# 🜂 H_A005 — Tame twister ("길들인 토네이도")

## Concept

A solar-heated ground plate launches a self-sustaining **atmospheric vortex engine** (a controlled,
anchored tornado). The vortex's natural updraft draws planetary air volumes through a compact
processing throat — turning the H_013 air-handling wall (~1.3e12 m³/yr for 1 Mt/yr) into *free
convection* powered by the sun-warmed surface, not fan electricity.

## Falsifiable prediction (kernel — not run)

The H_013 wall is the *energy* to move ~1.3e6 m³ air/ton past a contactor (fan power = volume × ΔP).
A vortex engine converts surface solar heating into mechanical draft at large scale. **Predict: the
vortex supplies the air-moving work at a fraction of fan electricity IF the contactor ΔP is kept low
(open mesh), but the throat residence time may be too short for capture** — the live kill criterion is
ΔP-vs-residence, not raw airflow. Vindicated if low-ΔP sorbent + vortex draft beats fan power per ton.

## Why novel (vs the verified H_0xx chain)

meteorological-scale contactor no level of the spec imagines.
