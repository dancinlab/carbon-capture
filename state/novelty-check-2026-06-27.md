## What IS novel here (the honest contribution)

1. **A single falsifiable bar for all of them** — every mechanism graded against the *same*
   thermodynamic floor and net-negativity criterion in one deterministic harness, so they are
   directly comparable. Most prior art evaluates each idea in its own bespoke frame.
2. **Quantitative kill/keep numbers** — e.g. centrifuge air-spin = 644× the floor; CO₂ frost
   point = −143 °C vs −50 °C passive cooling; enhanced weathering net-negative *even on a dirty
   grid* (+0.86) whereas electric DAC is futile there. These specific cross-comparable figures.
3. **The negative-space map** — showing the 6 FALSIFIED concepts share one failure (energy ≫ the
   separation floor), which sharpens *why* the live path is separation≈floor + swing-mode (H_019).

## Honest limits

- L1: "novelty" is judged at the *mechanism/principle* level. A specific engineering combination
  (e.g. vortex-draft + a named low-ΔP sorbent) could still be patentably novel — not assessed here.
- L2: prior-art coverage is a representative web/arxiv sweep, not an exhaustive patent search; an
  absence of a citation is "not found in this pass", not "proven non-existent".
- L3: re-deriving real fields from first principles is itself a useful sanity signal (the brainstorm
  was physically grounded), but it must NOT be mis-sold as discovery.
