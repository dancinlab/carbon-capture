## Family A — ACOUSTIC / MECHANICAL (grab by mass & momentum)

🎺 **A1 · ARIA-SCRUBBER** — "노래하는 탑"
- Mechanism: standing acoustic waves in a resonant column sort gas molecules by mass
  (acoustophoresis); CO₂ (44) drifts to pressure nodes away from N₂ (28)/O₂ (32).
- Falsifiable kernel: acoustic radiation force ∝ (Δdensity)·volume — is the CO₂/N₂ contrast
  enough to overcome diffusion at 400 ppm? (likely the kill criterion.)
- Novelty: zero sorbent, zero thermal swing — separation is a *sound field*, not a chemistry.

🌪️ **A2 · TAME-TWISTER** — "길들인 토네이도"
- Mechanism: a solar-heated ground plate launches a self-sustaining atmospheric vortex (vortex
  engine) that ingests planetary air volumes through a compact processing throat — directly
  attacking the H_013 air-handling wall (~1.3e12 m³/yr) with weather, not fans.
- Falsifiable kernel: vortex mechanical power vs the fan power to move the same air volume.
- Novelty: the contactor IS the weather; air handling becomes free convection.

🫧 **A3 · QUIET-BOIL** — "조용히 끓는 하늘"
- Mechanism: ultrasonic cavitation in a thin water film creates transient extreme micro-hotspots
  that fix dissolved CO₂ into carbonate without bulk heating.
- Falsifiable kernel: sonochemical yield per joule vs the carbonate formation enthalpy.
- Novelty: localized extreme conditions instead of bulk temperature/pressure swing.

🛰️ **A4 · CARBON-RING** — "탄소 원심 고리"
- Mechanism: an ultracentrifuge / orbital spinning ring separates CO₂ by molecular mass like
  isotope enrichment (44 vs 28/32) in a cascade.
- Falsifiable kernel: centrifuge separation factor α = exp(ΔM·v²/2RT) — how many stages to go
  400 ppm → useful? (the stage-count is the kill criterion → `H_A001`.)
- Novelty: gas-centrifuge separation applied to a trace species, no sorbent at all.

## Family B — ELECTROMAGNETIC (grab by charge & field)

⚡ **B1 · AURORA-COMB** — "오로라 빗"
- Mechanism: non-thermal plasma preferentially attaches electrons to CO₂ (positive electron
  affinity ~ forms CO₂⁻), then an electrostatic field combs the charged CO₂ out of neutral air.
- Falsifiable kernel: energy per ionization event vs the separation floor; recombination losses.
- Novelty: turns a neutral-gas separation into a charged-particle one (cheap to steer with fields).

🧲 **B2 · OXY-MAGNET** — "산소 자석"
- Mechanism: O₂ is strongly paramagnetic; CO₂/N₂ are not. A magnetic gradient pulls O₂ aside,
  *concentrating* CO₂+N₂, then a second stage handles the easier concentrated cut.
- Falsifiable kernel: magnetic force on O₂ (χ·∇B²) vs thermal motion — field strength needed.
- Novelty: separate by *magnetic susceptibility*, an axis no sorbent uses → `H_A004`.

🔌 **B3 · STATIC-NET** — "정전기 그물"
- Mechanism: wind-driven triboelectric mesh self-charges and electro-adsorbs polar/quadrupolar
  CO₂ passively, discharging to release — powered by wind motion, not a grid.
- Falsifiable kernel: triboelectric charge density vs CO₂ quadrupole binding energy.
- Novelty: passive, self-powered electro-capture from ambient wind.

💥 **B4 · PHOTON-CLEAVER** — "광자 가위"
- Mechanism: mode-selective IR laser climbs the CO₂ asymmetric-stretch vibrational ladder to
  directly photodissociate CO₂ → CO + O (or C + O₂), skipping capture entirely.
- Falsifiable kernel: photons·ℏω per molecule vs bond energy (5.5 eV) and laser wall-plug
  efficiency — almost certainly worse than capture, but quantifiable → `H_A003`.
- Novelty: destroy the molecule in mid-air instead of collecting it.

## Family C — PHOTONIC / QUANTUM (grab by light & coherence)

🔦 **C1 · SOLAR-BLINK** — "햇빛 깜빡이 흡착제"
- Mechanism: photoswitchable sorbent (azobenzene/spiropyran-grafted) binds CO₂ in one
  photo-isomer and releases in the other — regeneration by a *flash of light*, no thermal swing,
  directly removing the dominant H_010 sensible-heat term.
- Falsifiable kernel: photons per release cycle × ℏω vs the H_010 sensible heat it replaces → `H_A008`.
- Novelty: optical regeneration — the lever H_019 said matters, driven by light not heat.

🧊 **C2 · QUANTUM-TWEEZER** — "양자 집게"
- Mechanism: vibrational coherent control shapes a femtosecond pulse train to selectively lower
  the activation barrier for CO₂ chemisorption (coherent catalysis).
- Falsifiable kernel: coherence lifetime at 300 K vs the binding timescale (decoherence kill).
- Novelty: quantum coherence as the selectivity knob, not pore geometry.

🌈 **C3 · PRISM-SIEVE** — "프리즘 체"
- Mechanism: a photonic-crystal membrane whose band-gap resonates with CO₂'s polarizability
  slows/traps CO₂ by optical forces while N₂/O₂ pass.
- Falsifiable kernel: optical gradient force vs thermal velocity at the membrane.
- Novelty: a *light-structured* sieve, tunable by wavelength.

## Family D — THERMAL / RADIATIVE (grab by cold from the sky)

❄️ **D1 · NIGHT-FROST** — "밤서리 수집기"
- Mechanism: sky-facing radiative-cooling panels dump heat to deep space (≈3 K window) to reach
  the CO₂ frost point and snow CO₂ out — zero electricity, powered by the cold of the universe.
- Falsifiable kernel: CO₂ frost point at 0.42 mbar partial pressure (≈ −140 °C) vs the ≈ −50 °C
  floor of passive radiative cooling — likely FALSIFIED-in-principle, honestly → `H_A002`.
- Novelty: the heat sink is *space itself*; no compressor, no sorbent.

🏔️ **D2 · ALTITUDE-STILL** — "고도 증류탑"
- Mechanism: at high altitude / polar night, ambient is already cold; a tethered cryo-still
  fractionally freezes CO₂ from thin cold air with minimal added cooling.
- Falsifiable kernel: added cooling duty vs the compression-equivalent capture energy.
- Novelty: exploit the atmosphere's own cold as a free pre-cooler.

🧲 **D3 · MAGNETO-COOL** — "자기냉각 사다리"
- Mechanism: magnetocaloric cascade provides solid-state cooling (no compressor) to a cryo-capture
  stage — pairs with D1/D2.
- Falsifiable kernel: magnetocaloric ΔT per cycle vs the cooling span needed.
- Novelty: refrigeration by magnetic field cycling, not gas compression.

## Family E — BIO / LIVING (grab by metabolism)

🪸 **E1 · CARBON-CORAL** — "스스로 자라는 탄소 산호"
- Mechanism: engineered mineralizing organisms (carbonic-anhydrase-overexpressing) grow
  self-replicating carbonate/biochar megastructures from air + sunlight — the capture plant
  *builds itself*, sidestepping CAPEX.
- Falsifiable kernel: areal carbon-fixation rate (g/m²/day) × deployable area vs Gt/yr target.
- Novelty: a von-Neumann-style self-replicating capture substrate; CAPEX → biology.

🧱 **E2 · BREATHING-STONE** — "숨쉬는 콘크리트"
- Mechanism: living building materials (cyanobacteria + carbonic anhydrase in a hydrogel matrix)
  turn every wall/road into a passive CO₂→carbonate sink — cities as the contactor.
- Falsifiable kernel: enzyme turnover × accessible surface vs urban CO₂ flux → `H_A007`.
- Novelty: capture distributed into existing infrastructure surface area (no new plants).

🌊 **E3 · ENZYME-KELP** — "효소 켈프 숲"
- Mechanism: gene-printed kelp/algae megafarms with engineered RuBisCO-bypass fixation deliver
  captured carbon to the deep ocean (gravity sequestration).
- Falsifiable kernel: net fixation minus ocean re-equilibration (the H_009 ocean wall) — does it
  net-remove?
- Novelty: planetary biological pump as the storage path, not geology.

## Family F — ELECTROCHEMICAL (grab by electrons)

🔋 **F1 · CARBON-BATTERY** — "탄소 배터리"
- Mechanism: a redox-active quinone electrode binds CO₂ when *charged* and releases when
  *discharged* — capture and energy storage are the same device; charge from curtailed renewables,
  the grid's surplus becomes captured carbon.
- Falsifiable kernel: electrons per CO₂ × cell voltage = energy vs the floor → `H_A006`.
- Novelty: electro-swing with NO temperature/pressure swing AND doubling as grid storage.

⚗️ **F2 · pH-PUMP** — "pH 시소"
- Mechanism: bipolar-membrane electrodialysis swings local pH to absorb CO₂ (high pH) then strip
  it (low pH) — an electrochemical analog of the moisture swing, fully electric.
- Falsifiable kernel: electrodialysis energy per mol H⁺ cycled vs CO₂ captured.
- Novelty: chemistry-free swing driven purely by ion transport.
